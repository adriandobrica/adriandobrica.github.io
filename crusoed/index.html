Idea

Crusoed is a 2D puzzle/adventure/survival game for the PC, inspired by Robinson Crusoe’s adventure on a lonely island. The stranded player must try to survive on the island, by gathering various objects and combining them to create more performant objects, which may aid him in his attempt to leave the island and return to civilization.

Key Features

  • over 40 items to collect and craft
  • five huge islands to explore (100 x 100 tiles each)
  • a survival system based on health, hunger, thirst and sleep
  • over 10 puzzles to complete
